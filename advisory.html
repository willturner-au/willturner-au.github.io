The trusted advisor model

This is not consulting. There's no team, no report, no 90-day engagement. There is Will — available, embedded, and completely invested in your organisation's clarity and momentum.

The trusted advisor relationship works because it operates outside the politics of your organisation. Will has no agenda, no team to protect, and nothing to sell. His only job is to help you see clearly and move forward.

Will limits this to two to three organisations at a time. That scarcity is deliberate — it's what makes the depth possible.
