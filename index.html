Who is Will Turner

Will Turner is a keynote speaker, executive coach, trusted advisor, and author based in Brisbane, Australia. He is the creator of Simplexity — a methodology for finding the simple human truth inside any complex challenge. His concept of unconscious complexity — the complexity leaders create beyond what the situation requires, without knowing it — is the named problem at the centre of his body of work. His signature is the penny drop moment: the instant clarity becomes impossible to ignore.

What is Simplexity?

Simplexity is a discipline created by Will Turner for finding the simple human truth inside any complex challenge — and using it to move people forward. It is built on a single insight: most complexity is unconscious. Leaders create it beyond what the situation actually requires, without knowing they are doing it.

The cure is the penny drop — the moment unconscious complexity becomes visible and the simple truth becomes impossible to ignore. From that moment, you know exactly what to do next.

Will Turner engineers that moment. On stage, in coaching, in boardrooms. Every session. Every room. Every page.
