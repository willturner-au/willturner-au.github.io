About Will Turner as a speaker

Will Turner is a Brisbane-based keynote speaker with more than 20 years of experience delivering to leadership audiences across Australia, Asia-Pacific, and internationally. He is the creator of Simplexity — a discipline for finding the simple human truth inside any complex challenge — and of the concept of unconscious complexity, which describes the complexity leaders create beyond what the situation actually requires.

His signature keynote has been delivered to leadership teams in financial services, healthcare, technology, retail, professional services, government, and education. The methodology works across every sector because it is built on a universal human truth: complexity is almost always a choice, and so is clarity.

Will is available for conference keynotes, leadership offsites, internal all-hands events, and facilitator-led workshops. He works directly with event organisers and leadership teams to customise every session to the specific challenge the audience is facing.
