The keynote

Will's signature keynote — Simplexity — works for any industry, any leadership audience, at any level. It starts with a familiar human moment, uses it to reveal the unconscious complexity in the room, and ends with every person knowing exactly one thing they need to do differently.

That's the penny drop. And it's different every time — because Will customises every keynote to the audience and context. Never a repeat performance. Always the same result.

Will also speaks on leadership under complexity, the art of clear communication, and decision-making when everything feels hard. Every talk is built on the same foundation: find the human truth, create the penny drop, leave the room changed.
