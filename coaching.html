Executive Coaching

High value.
The penny drop. 1:1.

Will works with a small number of senior leaders at a time — one-on-one, deeply, over time. He helps them see the unconscious complexity they've created, find the simple truth inside it, and leave every session knowing exactly what to do next.

How Will coaches

Most leaders have a clarity problem disguised as a complexity problem. They're too close to see what's actually simple — trapped in unconscious complexity beyond what the situation requires.

Will's coaching creates the penny drop in private. Session by session, he helps leaders move from unconscious complexity to conscious simplicity — until they can find the simple truth themselves.

Sessions are direct, challenging, and free of the language that makes coaching feel like coaching. This is not a programme or a package. It's a relationship — as long as it's useful, and not a day longer.

"Will doesn't ask you to think harder. He shows you where you've been thinking wrong."
Executive coaching client, ASX-listed company
Who this is for
The leader who's stuck
You know something needs to change but can't see what. You're creating unconscious complexity — and you need someone to show you where.
The high performer at a ceiling
You've succeeded by doing what you've always done. The next level requires something simpler — and you're not sure what that is yet.
The leader needing a thinking partner
Smart people around you, but nobody who will tell you the simple truth. Will is that person — direct, trusted, entirely in your corner.
The executive in transition
New role, new complexity. You need the penny drop quickly — before people have decided who you are.
What to expect
01
A frank first conversation
Will starts by understanding what's actually going on — not what you think is going on. Expect direct questions and the first penny drop moments from session one.
02
Regular 1:1 sessions
Fortnightly or monthly. Structured around what matters right now — not a predetermined curriculum. The penny drop shows up differently every time.
03
Between-session access
When something comes up between sessions — and it will — Will is available. A real relationship, not a calendar slot.
