Executive Coaching

High value.
Low volume.

Will works with a small number of senior leaders at a time — one-on-one, deeply, over time. Not a programme. Not a framework. A genuine thinking partnership for leaders who are ready to get out of their own way.

How Will coaches

Most coaching asks you to work harder on the right things. Will's approach is different: he helps you see what you can't currently see — the complexity you've created, the assumptions you're carrying, the simple truth you're too close to find.

Sessions are direct, challenging, and free of the language that makes coaching feel like coaching. Will brings the same clarity to the room that he brings to the stage — and he won't let you hide behind complexity.

This is not a subscription or a package. It's a relationship — as long as it's useful, and not a day longer.

"Will doesn't ask you to think harder. He shows you where you've been thinking wrong."

Who this is for
The senior leader who's stuck
You know something needs to change but can't see what. You're too close to the problem — and the people around you are too polite to tell you.
The high performer hitting a ceiling
You've succeeded by doing what you've always done. But the next level requires something different — and you're not sure what that is yet.
The leader who needs a thinking partner
You're surrounded by smart people but there's no one you can truly think out loud with. Will is that person — direct, trusted, and entirely in your corner.
The executive in transition
New role, new context, new complexity. You need to find the simple truth quickly — and move people before they've decided who you are.
What to expect
01
A frank first conversation
Will starts by understanding what's actually going on — not what you think is going on. Expect direct questions and honest observations from the first session.
02
Regular 1:1 sessions
Fortnightly or monthly, depending on what's useful. Sessions are structured around what matters right now — not a predetermined curriculum.
03
Between-session access
When something comes up between sessions — and it will — Will is available. A short message, a quick call. This is a real relationship, not a calendar slot.
